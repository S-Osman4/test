About Us

Our team comes with the experience and knowledge

Skyhaven Care LLC is a Behavioral Health Residential Facility, that provides counseling and assistance in self-administration of medication. We offer a comprehensive and specialized approach to Mental Health and Substance Abuse disorder recovery. We understand the unique challenges faced by individuals in their journey towards mental wellness and have designed our program to address each person's specific needs.


Our residential facility provides a safe and supportive environment where clients can focus solely on their recovery. This dedicated time and space to allow for a deep exploration of underlying issues and the development of coping mechanisms and skills necessary for long-term recovery. Clients have access to a team of experienced professionals who provide personalized therapy, counseling, and support. We provide short-term services to mental health and co- occurring seriously mentally ill/substance abuse males and females with minimal medical issues. This facility operates 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Our mission is to strengthen our community by providing person-centered care to individuals impacted by Mental health, and Substance Abuse disorders, and other behavioral through a comprehensive system of educations, prevention, intervention, and counseling.

Our Philosophy is to help our patients heal not only physically, but also mentally, and spiritually, so that when they leave our facility, they're able to live a full, healthy life, without relapse.
